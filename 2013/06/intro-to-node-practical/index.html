Node.js Cape Town

June Meetup

 18 June 2013

Follow up to our first meetup about the Intro to NodeJS is happening on 19 June 2013 at Acceleration, 2nd Floor, Longkloof Studios, Darters Road, Gardens.

Presentations

  • Practical Introduction to NodeJS by Simon Bishop

    Learning NodeJS by building a Clustered Network of NodeJS Instances. In a hour or two !

Simon Bishop is presenting a Practical Introduction to NodeJS. This session will focus on building a actual NodeJS Clustered System. That will show the power of NodeJS. Think multiple Nodes talking to each other with a very cool visual presentation of the connected Nodes. Should be great !

Acceleration has provided the venue.

KeyConnections has graciously sponsored free Pizza for the event.

We will try our best to find a sponsor for some Beer and Drinks.

Doors will open from 18:00 and we will try to get started at the latest by 19:00, If you’re early pop by and have a drink with us. We plan to wrap up by 21:00 at the latest.

These meetings are a great way to meet other developers who have similar interests, and offer amazing opportunities to learn from each other. Also, they are a great excuse to get out of the house and socialize.

We hope to see you there!
